About Us

Established in the year 2000, "Master Enterprises", we are Manufacturer, Wholesaler, Trader, Retailer and Service Provider of Fusing Machine, Fusing Machine Belts, Conveyor Belt, Digital Thermometer and many more. These belts are unmatched in terms of quality and performance and are largely used in various sectors. In addition, we also provide prompt and reliable Repair and Maintenance Services for the Fusing Machine manufactured by us. Being a trader we trade our products with the brand name of Rollax, QWB, Resin and many more.

Under the able guidance and support of our owner, Mr. S. P. Sheshachala, we have been able to grow in leaps and bounds in a short period of time. He holds a rich industrial experience for many years, and holds an in-depth knowledge in production and marketing sectors, which has helped us offer products that match the needs and expectations of our clients.

With the support of highly talented professionals and a robust infrastructure, we are able to cater to the increasing demands of our clients successfully. The manufacturing unit is well equipped with all the necessities that further enable us to handle bulk orders in a timely manner. Moreover, our team of experts is highly professional and dedicated and work in close coordination to enable a smooth production process. The quality controllers conduct tests on various parameters on each and every product before its dispatch to ensure that our clients receive a flawless and defect free range. We also assure our clients that these products are made from cutting edge technology and are in compliance with the international standards of quality. By offering high quality products, timely delivery, world class services, customized solutions and affordable pricing, we have carved a niche for ourselves in the market.
